Community Guidelines

By posting on Catalyst Forums you agree to follow these rules.

1. Be respectful

Treat everyone with respect.

2. No hate speech or slurs

Racial slurs are not allowed in any form.

3. Swearing

Casual swearing is fine. Swearing at someone may result in a ban.

4. Sexual content

Inappropriate sexual content will result in punishment.

5. No threats or calls for harm

Threatening language results in an immediate ban.

6. No spam or advertising

Repeated posting or unapproved advertising results in a ban.

7. False Information

Using inspect element or photoshop to change a person's post and defame them will result in a ban.

Dev Note

Content the developer finds inappropriate may be removed.
